Websites for shopping cubes

  • Rubik's official website

    If you want to buy the official Rubik's brand Rubik's cubes, this is the best website. However, Rubik's brand is not the best for speedcubing as its cubes are known for their stiffness and little cornercutting.

  • Amazon

    If you know exactly which brand or type of cubes you want to get, Amazon is about the best website to buy cubes. You can often find low price cubes with good quality.

  • The Cubicle

    This website is for cubers who are more experienced. It offers all kinds of speedcubes as well as cubing accessories such as stickers, lubricants, timers, etc.

  • Lightake

    This website is similar to the Cubicle except it doesn't specialize in cubes. However, there are some cheap and good cubes on there.

If you're not sure which speedcube you want to get, check out speedsolving.com (in which you can find tons of information about cubing) or this video (in which LACuber lists some of the characteristics of the most popular speedcubes on the market).
