About this website

Introduction

This website took about 20 hours in total to finish and was finalized in December 2016. Because this is the first time I make a website, a lot of times I faced trouble making what I want.

Challenges

My original idea of making a website was not about the Rubik's cube. Later, an incident happened, and it made me change my plan. Finally I decided to make a website about the Rubik's cube. When I started, due to my limited knowledge of HTML and CSS, it was very hard for me to position all the elements at the places I want them to be. Later on, it became easier and easier, but still I was not able to complete everything I wanted to put on my website.

What I have learned

  • HTML and CSS languages
  • How to play around GitHub and the terminal
  • That Google is a great tool when facing difficulties
  • That the Internet is a great place and that all of us should utilize it because it can give us tremendous advantages
